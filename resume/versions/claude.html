About

I'm an SRE at Okta with six years building and operating the Workflows platform — a no-code automation product that sits in the critical path for enterprise customers. My work is at the intersection of software engineering and operations: I write code to make systems easier to run, design observability that surfaces the right signals, and try to understand failure modes well enough that the next incident is genuinely less likely to recur.

I hold a Master's in Data Analytics Engineering from Northeastern (Fulbright Scholar) and am actively moving toward ML engineering — not as a career pivot, but as a natural extension of the infrastructure work I already do. Systems that run ML in production still need to be reliable, observable, and operable. That's where I'm headed.
